Zone Model for Prioritization and Expectation Alignment
The Zone Model is a prioritization framework. You classify your agent's capabilities into three zones based on Priority + Value + Effort — how much your team is willing to invest in each and how important each is.
Zone 1: This Must Work Highest value functions. Failures are costly or unacceptable. Full investment, clear acceptance criteria. Eval cadence: every change.
Zone 2: Real but Lower Priority Scoped capabilities that don't receive full effort this phase. Lower expectations are an intentional business decision. Eval cadence: before releases.
Zone 3: Exploratory Coverage from real-world usage — unexpected patterns, new tools. Research and exploration. Watch for rising usage and promote upward. Eval cadence: periodic.
