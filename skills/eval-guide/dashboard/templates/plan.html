Value × Risk Matrix
Each acceptance criterion is placed in one of four quadrants based on Value (how much does getting it right drive the product?) and Risk (how much harm does a failure cause?). Drag criteria between quadrants as your thinking evolves.
High Value · High Risk Product-defining capabilities. Most test cases, strictest review.
High Value · Low Risk Expected capabilities users rely on. Occasional misses tolerable.
Low Value · High Risk Rarely triggered but must never fail. Safety, compliance, refusals.
Low Value · Low Risk Exploratory or rare. Light coverage; revisit if usage grows.
